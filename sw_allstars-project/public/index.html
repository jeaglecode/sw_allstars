1st Annual SouthWest AllStars Regional Tournament

2020 SouthWest AllStars Baseball Tournament

Thank you for choosing to play in the 2020 SouthWest AllStars Baseball Tournament. At SouthWest Allstars we care about creating an safe and welcoming environment for all our players. The tournament will take place November 14-15, 2020. It is open to all kids ages 4-14 and will be split up into age groups. Team managers can register their team and players using our online portal. Parents can register with a code from their managers. Deadline to register for event must be by November 4, 2020. All donations and proceeds go to the Boys and Girls foundation. Thank you, have fun and play ball!

Divisons/Ages

T-Ball: 4-6

Minors: 7-9

Majors: 10-12

Juniors: 12-14
